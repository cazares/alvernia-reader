# Mixterious Copyright and DMCA Policy (Draft)

Effective date: 2026-02-19  
Owner: Miguel Cazares (App Owner)

## 1) Policy Scope

- Mixterious respects intellectual property rights and responds to valid infringement notices.
- Evidence required: Publicly reachable policy URL and internal notice intake procedure.

## 2) Notice Submission Requirements

A copyright notice must include:

1. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed.
2. Identification of the allegedly infringing material and sufficient locating information.
3. Contact information of the complaining party.
4. A good-faith statement that use is unauthorized.
5. A statement under penalty of perjury that the notice is accurate and the sender is authorized.
6. Physical or electronic signature.

Evidence required: Notice intake form/template used by support/legal.

## 4) Counter-Notice Requirements

A counter-notice must include:

1. Identification of removed/disabled material and prior location.
2. Statement under penalty of perjury of good-faith belief in error/misidentification.
3. Consent to applicable jurisdiction and service of process.
4. Physical or electronic signature.

Evidence required: Counter-notice template and legal review approval.

## 5) Repeat Infringer Policy

- Mixterious may suspend or terminate access for repeat infringement or clear abuse.
- Evidence required: Documented repeat-infringer threshold and enforcement log procedure.
